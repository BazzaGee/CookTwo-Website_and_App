# Tier 5 — Emerging & Family Organizers

**Threat Level Range:** LOW → LOW-MEDIUM  
**Competitors:** 8  
**Status:** All live, all early-stage or adjacent

## Overview

This tier contains early-stage startups, hobby projects, and family organizers with peripheral relevance to Cupla. Healthspan is conceptually closest to Cupla (couples nutrition) but has zero market traction. OurPlate and MiseBook are early-stage indie products with limited tech. SummitPlate is the most commercially serious (freemium SaaS) but targets families. Cookbond has a unique Me/You/Us role system worth studying. WhatDinner and We Just Did Dinner are negligible. Cozi has 17M+ users but the shallowest meal planning of all competitors.

## Key Takeaway for Cupla

Healthspan is the only competitor in this tier with genuine couples positioning — but they have zero users and are stuck in open beta. Cookbond's Me/You/Us role system is worth studying for Cupla's Cook Mode design. Cozi has massive distribution (17M users) but meal planning is a tiny feature in a family organizer app. The remaining competitors are negligible. **No competitor in this tier threatens Cupla's positioning or execution.**
