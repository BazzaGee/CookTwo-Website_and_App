## PART A: CONTENT STRATEGY

### 1. TikTok Content Formats (Post 1x/day)

#### Format 1: "My Plate vs His Plate" (Highest Viral Potential)
**Hook:** "Same dinner. Different plates. [Show both plates side by side]"
**Structure:**
1. Show the shared cooking process (5 seconds)
2. Split screen: Partner A's plate vs Partner B's plate (3 seconds)
3. Show Cupla app screen with calorie/macro breakdown for each (3 seconds)
4. Text overlay: "One meal. Two plates. Both hit their goals."
**Hashtags:** #myplatevshisplate #cookingfortwo #mealprep #fitnesscouple #cuplaapp

#### Format 2: "What's in Our Pantry → What the AI Suggests"
**Hook:** "We have chicken, rice, spinach, and half an onion. Watch what Cupla makes..."
**Structure:**
1. Quick pantry shot (3 seconds)
2. Type ingredients into Cupla (3 seconds)
3. AI generates meal — reaction (3 seconds)
4. Cook and plate (5 seconds)
**Hashtags:** #mealplanning #cookingfortwo #whatshouldimake #pantry #cuplaapp

#### Format 3: "Grocery Haul → Shared List Sync"
**Hook:** "We synced our grocery list in real-time for the first time..."
**Structure:**
1. Partner A adds items at home (3 seconds)
2. Partner B sees them appear at store in real-time (3 seconds)
3. Check off items — both see updates (3 seconds)
4. "Move to Pantry" — cycle starts again (3 seconds)
**Hashtags:** #groceryhaul #cookingfortwo #couplegoals #mealplanning #cuplaapp

#### Format 4: "Stop the Argument"
**Hook:** "We used to spend 20 minutes every night arguing about dinner. Now it takes 5 minutes."
**Structure:**
1. Act out the "what do you want for dinner?" argument (5 seconds)
2. Show Cupla generating the meal plan (3 seconds)
3. Both partners cooking together (3 seconds)
4. Happy couple eating (3 seconds)
**Hashtags:** #couplegoals #mealplanning #relationship #cookingfortwo #cuplaapp

#### Format 5: "5-Step Sunday Reset"
**Hook:** "Our 5-minute Sunday routine that saves us 5 hours a week"
**Structure:**
1. Open Cupla (1 second)
2. Review pantry (1 second)
3. Generate week's meals (1 second)
4. Missing items → grocery list (1 second)
5. Both partners approve (1 second)
**Hashtags:** #sundayreset #mealprep #cookingfortwo #weeklyplanning #cuplaapp

## PART B: PARTNERSHIP PLAYBOOK

### Tier 1 Partnerships (Weeks 1-4)

#### 1. Micro-Influencer Barter Program

**What you offer:**
- Free lifetime Cupla premium access (value: ~$96/year)
- 20% affiliate revenue share on any signups from their unique link
- Early access to new features + input on product direction
- Featured as "Cupla Creator Partner" on website (social proof)

**What you ask for:**
- 1-2 authentic TikTok/IG posts showing how they use Cupla
- Honest review — no scripted content
- Tag @cuplaapp and use #cuplaapp hashtag

**Target:** 8 creators from Tier 1 influencer list (all under 50K, all post exact Cupla-format content)

**Outreach channel:** TikTok DM (personalized, referencing specific video)

**Expected response rate:** 30-50% (4 of 8 should say yes)

---

#### 2. Fair Play Life / Eve Rodsky Ecosystem

**What Fair Play is:** Eve Rodsky's "Fair Play" method divides 100 household task cards between partners. Meal planning is one of the 100 cards. The Fair Play community actively discusses how to divide meal planning labor.

**What you offer:**
- Cupla as the practical tool for the "meal planning" card
- Free Cupla access for Fair Play community members
- Co-branded content: "How Cupla helps you play fair in the kitchen"

**How to approach:**
- Email via fairplaylife.com contact form
- Reference specific Fair Play card (Meal Planning)
- Offer Cupla as the tool that makes the card easier to share

**Likelihood:** Medium — Fair Play is open to tools that reduce invisible labor

---

#### 3. Reddit Community Building

**Strategy:** Build genuine presence before launching

**Weeks 1-2: Karma building**
- Create dedicated Cupla Reddit account
- Comment helpfully on 5+ threads per day across 10 subreddits
- Never mention Cupla — just be helpful
- Track karma growth (target: 500+ karma in 2 weeks)

**Weeks 3-4: Soft mentions**
- When someone asks "what app do you use for X?", mention Cupla as one option among several
- Share blog posts as helpful resources (10:1 ratio)
- Answer "cooking for different diets" questions with Cupla-specific advice

**Weeks 5+: Launch post**
- Post "I built a couples food app" to r/SideProject
- Cross-post to r/startups
- Share genuine story, not marketing pitch
