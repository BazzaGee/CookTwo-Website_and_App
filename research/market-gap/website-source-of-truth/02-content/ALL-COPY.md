## FEATURES OVERVIEW PAGE

**Headline:** Designed for "We", not "You"

**Subheadline:** Discover how our intuitive features make planning, shopping, and cooking together a breeze.

### Feature: Adaptive Shared Cooking (Deep Dive)

**Badge:** Signature Feature

**Title:** Adaptive Shared Cooking

**Body:** One cooking session, two different plates. We generate a unified recipe with AI-adapted plating instructions so you both hit your unique goals without cooking twice.

**Detailed Body:**
When you share a life, you share meals. But traditional meal planning apps treat everyone as an isolated individual. They expect you to either abandon your goals to eat the same thing, or cook completely separate dinners.

We solved this. You follow one single cooking workflow together. When the food is ready, the app provides visual, AI-adapted plating guides. It tells you exactly how much chicken to give Partner A, and how much extra rice to give Partner B.

It eliminates friction, reduces dishes, and brings the joy back to a shared kitchen.

**Example:**
- Partner A (weight loss): ~450 cal — chicken over bed of spinach, ½ cup rice
- Partner B (muscle gain): ~700 cal — extra chicken over full cup of rice
- Same stir-fry. Same pan. Different plates.

## FAQ PAGE

**Headline:** Frequently Asked Questions

**Subheadline:** Everything you need to know to get your shared kitchen running smoothly.

### Q: Is the beta really free?
Yes! Couples Food System is completely free during our Beta period. No credit card required. We just want your feedback to make the app perfect.

### Q: Do we need two separate accounts?
You'll each have your own login, but they link together securely to form one Shared Kitchen. This allows for individual goal-setting while keeping planning and shopping 100% collaborative.

### Q: How does Adaptive Plating work?
You cook the exact same meal in one pan. At the end, our AI gives you visual plating guides — for example, suggesting Partner A takes a larger portion of protein, while Partner B adds extra rice and greens.

### Q: What if we have the same dietary goals?
Even better! The app works perfectly for couples with identical goals too. You'll still benefit from shared grocery lists, pantry-aware AI, and the collaborative planning experience.

### Q: What platforms are supported?
Couples Food System works on any device with a web browser — iPhone, Android, tablet, or desktop. No app store download needed. Just open the link and start planning.

### Q: How is our data protected?
We take privacy seriously. Your dietary data, health goals, and personal information are encrypted and never shared with third parties. Period.

### Q: Will it always be free?
During Beta, everything is free. After launch, core collaboration features (shared lists, basic planning) will remain free. Advanced AI features may become part of a premium tier — but Beta users will get special early access pricing.
