Questions & Clarity

Everything you need to know about ritualizing your kitchen culture. We’re here to help you and your partner navigate the Sunday Morning experience.

What is the "Sunday Morning" method?

expand_more
The method is a collaborative meal-planning framework designed specifically for couples. Instead of a robotic shopping list, we focus on a shared 20-minute ritual each Sunday morning where you co-create your week's nourishment. It's about presence, not just grocery lists.

How does "Shared Kitchen" sync work?

expand_more
Both partners have full visibility. When one person adds an ingredient or checks off a meal, it updates instantly for the other. We use a low-latency collaborative engine so you never buy the same gallon of milk twice.

Is the Beta open to everyone?

expand_more
We are currently in a "Closed Beta" for our first 500 couples. We release new invite codes every Friday morning. Joining the waitlist is the best way to get priority access as we scale our infrastructure.

How is our dietary data protected?

expand_more
Your data is yours. We encrypt all shared notes and meal histories. We never sell your dietary preferences or shopping habits to third-party advertisers. We believe your kitchen should remain a private sanctuary.

Does it integrate with Google Calendar?

expand_more
Yes! You can export your "Cook Rituals" directly to Google, Apple, or Outlook calendars so your dinner dates are clearly blocked out in your shared schedule.
