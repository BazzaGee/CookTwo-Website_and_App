Transforming meal planning into a shared ritual.

Born from the kitchen of two humans who wanted to cook better, together. Sunday Morning is a philosophy of collaborative, adaptive nutrition.

THE PROBLEM

Nutrition shouldn't be a solo mission.

Most fitness apps are designed for the individual—the "me" instead of the "us." They track data point-by-point, calorie-by-calorie, turning food into a math problem.

We realized that for couples, the biggest friction isn't what to eat, but the cognitive load of deciding together. We wanted to build something that honors the shared experience of eating.
