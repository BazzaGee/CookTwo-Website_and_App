How Sunday Morning Works

Transform the chaos of meal planning into a serene, shared ritual. Our five-step method is designed to bring you closer through the art of nourishment.

1

Invite Partner

Create your shared digital kitchen and sync with your partner's calendar. No more "what's for dinner?" texts at 5 PM.

2

Set Goals

Define your weekly budget, dietary preferences, and energy levels. We align your menu with your actual lifestyle.

Low Effort Weekdays Healthy Dinners Plant-Forward
3

Stock Pantry

Snap a photo of your shelves. Our AI inventories what you have, ensuring nothing goes to waste and shopping is simplified.

4

Generate Meals

Every Sunday morning, receive a curated plan. Review together, swap recipes, and approve your week in one click.

"This week: Lemon Herb Salmon for Tuesday, Sweet Potato Curry for Thursday."
5

Cook Together

Follow the split-task instructions. One preps, one sautés. It’s not just dinner; it’s quality time rediscovered.
