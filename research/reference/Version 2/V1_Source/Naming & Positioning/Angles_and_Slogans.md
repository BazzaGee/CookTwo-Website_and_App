## 11. Recommended Combinations

### Option A: The "One Dinner" Approach
- **Name:** One Dinner (or Two Plates)
- **Tagline:** "Same meal. Two plates."
- **Marketing hook:** "The app that ends the 'what's for dinner' argument."
- **TikTok angle:** "How we eat the exact same dinner, but he gains muscle and I lose weight."

### Option B: The "Shared Kitchen" Approach
- **Name:** Shared Kitchen (or KitchenSync)
- **Tagline:** "Your shared kitchen. Smarter."
- **Marketing hook:** "Plan meals together without the mental load."
- **TikTok angle:** "Stop doing macro math for two people."

### Option C: The "Eat Together" Approach
- **Name:** Eat Together (or Feed)
- **Tagline:** "Eat together. Differently."
- **Marketing hook:** "What are WE eating tonight?"
- **TikTok angle:** "Cook once. Plate twice."

### Option D: The "We Decide" Approach
- **Name:** WeCook (or Our Dinner)
- **Tagline:** "We do the planning. You do the cooking."
- **Marketing hook:** "The app that decides dinner together."
- **TikTok angle:** "Two goals. One dinner. Zero arguments."
