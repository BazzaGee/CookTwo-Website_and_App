# Tier 4 — AI Meal Planning

**Threat Level Range:** LOW → MEDIUM  
**Competitors:** 8  
**Status:** All live, ranging from hobby projects to 6M-user platforms

## Overview

This tier contains apps focused on AI-powered meal planning and recipe management — the "AI chef" layer. Samsung Food is the heavyweight (6M users, Samsung ecosystem moat, 240K recipes) but lacks couples features. Ollie AI is the closest functional competitor (90K families, AI-native) but deliberately ignores couples in favor of families. The remaining competitors are individual-focused meal planners with varying AI depth.

## Key Takeaway for Cupla

Samsung Food is the sleeping giant — 6M users, Samsung ecosystem integration, and the resources to add any feature. However, they lack couples positioning and their AI recommendations reportedly don't reflect dietary preferences. Ollie AI validates AI-native meal planning but targets families, not couples. Cooklist validates pantry-first demand but is self-destructing via B2B pivot. Melio has the deepest AI pipeline (7-stage LangGraph) but is nutrition-first, not relationship-first. **Cupla's AI advantage is in the combination of pantry-first + adaptive portions + relationship context.**
