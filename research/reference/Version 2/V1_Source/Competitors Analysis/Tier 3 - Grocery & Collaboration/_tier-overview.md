# Tier 3 — Grocery & Collaboration

**Threat Level Range:** LOW → LOW-MEDIUM  
**Competitors:** 4  
**Status:** All live, all established

## Overview

This tier contains apps focused on shared grocery lists and shopping collaboration — the "list sync" layer of the food planning stack. AnyList is the gold standard for shared grocery lists with recipe import and Apple pedigree. OurGroceries is the simplest pure list sync tool with widest voice assistant coverage. Listonic has the most scale (20M downloads, 40+ languages) with shallow AI features. KitchenSync is a tiny player but validates couples monetization with its $8.99/mo Duo tier.

## Key Takeaway for Cupla

These competitors own the grocery list sync layer but nothing beyond it. AnyList is the benchmark for shared list UX — study their real-time sync implementation. KitchenSync's $8.99/mo Duo tier validates that couples will pay for shared food tools. However, none have meal planning intelligence, pantry tracking, or adaptive portions. Cupla's grocery list sync should match AnyList's quality while layering on pantry-first AI and adaptive portions.
