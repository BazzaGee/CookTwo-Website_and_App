# Tier 2 — Nutrition Intelligence

**Threat Level Range:** LOW-MEDIUM → NONE  
**Competitors:** 2  
**Status:** 1 established player, 1 non-existent

## Overview

This tier contains apps focused on nutrition tracking and macro intelligence rather than couples meal planning. Eat This Much is a well-established individual macro meal planner with 6M+ users. Nutrola is an AI photo nutrition tracker with 2M+ claimed users but no meal planning or couples features. Neither has adaptive portions, real-time sync, or relationship-first UX.

## Competitor Summary

| Competitor | Threat | Status | Pricing | Users | Key Feature |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Eat This Much** | LOW-MEDIUM | Live (web+iOS+Android) | $6.99/mo or $39.99/yr | 6M+ | Auto-generated macro meal plans |
| **Nutrola** | LOW-MEDIUM | Live (iOS+Android) | Freemium | 2M+ claimed | AI photo nutrition tracking |

## Key Takeaway for Cupla

These competitors validate the AI meal generation and nutrition tracking market but operate in entirely different lanes. Eat This Much is individual-focused (no shared features). Nutrola is a photo tracker (no meal planning). Neither threatens Cupla's couples positioning. However, both have large user bases — if either pivots to couples/shared features, they'd have significant distribution advantage.
