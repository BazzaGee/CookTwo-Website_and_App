# Tier 1 — Direct Couples & Shared Food

**Threat Level Range:** LOW → VERY HIGH  
**Competitors:** 9  
**Status:** 3 live with significant traction, 4 early-stage, 2 pre-launch/hobby

## Overview

This tier contains the most dangerous competitors — apps that directly target the same problem Cupla solves: couples or households with different dietary needs wanting to cook together. Yummo and Fitia are the primary threats. Yummo owns the "one cook, two plates" concept with a live product and $9.99/mo pricing. Fitia has 10M+ users and is actively adding shared/family features. PlateMates and SplitPlate have near-identical positioning but are early-stage. The remaining competitors (Cloche, OttoChef, DuoDine, slrp) have partial concept overlap but lack the full feature set.

## Key Takeaway for Cupla

**Yummo is the single most dangerous competitor** — they own the same conceptual territory ("one cook, two plates") with a live, monetized product. Fitia is the distribution threat — 10M users and actively adding shared features. Cupla must ship real-time sync + relationship UX before either closes those gaps. The remaining 7 competitors validate market demand but lack the execution to be immediate threats.
